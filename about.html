Our Story

From humble beginnings to Ghana's leading smart home security provider

Our Mission

At Safety Pro Installations, our mission is to revolutionize home security across Ghana by providing state-of-the-art smart home systems that enhance safety, efficiency, and comfort. We believe every family deserves to feel secure in their own home.

Making smart homes accessible to every Ghanaian family

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and innovative smart home security company in West Africa, setting the standard for excellence in installation, customer service, and technological advancement.

Leading the smart home revolution in West Africa

Founded in 2022

Safety Pro Installations was born from a simple observation: Ghanaian homes needed better security solutions that were both advanced and affordable. Our founders, with backgrounds in technology and security, set out to bridge this gap.

Rapid Growth

In just three years, we've secured over 500 homes across Accra, Tema, and surrounding areas. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us the go-to choice for smart home security.
